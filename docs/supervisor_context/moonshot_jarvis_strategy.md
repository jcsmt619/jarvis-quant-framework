# Moonshot Jarvis Strategy Context

Moonshot Jarvis is a research-only strategy track.

It is not a live trading system.

## Purpose

Moonshot Jarvis studies asymmetric, convex opportunities such as:
- long-dated LEAPS
- optionality
- major trend/catalyst setups
- high-upside equity ideas
- paper portfolio scenarios
- risk/reward research

## Key principle

Moonshot is for research, monitoring, and paper simulation first.

It must not:
- auto-buy options
- auto-buy shares
- submit orders
- enable live trading
- override human approval
- touch broker credentials

## Desired outputs

Moonshot systems may produce:
- candidate lists
- thesis memos
- risk/reward tables
- catalyst calendars
- valuation notes
- option-chain quality checks
- LEAPS watchlists
- paper portfolio reports
- risk warnings
- “human review required” alerts

## Required labels

Moonshot outputs should use safety labels:
- RESEARCH_ONLY
- MONITOR_ONLY
- PAPER_ONLY
- HUMAN_REVIEW_REQUIRED
- BLOCKED_BY_SAFETY_GATE

## Promotion idea

Moonshot ideas can only move forward through:
1. research memo
2. strategy card
3. experiment registry
4. backtest or scenario test when possible
5. paper portfolio tracking
6. risk review
7. explicit human approval

No automatic live execution.
